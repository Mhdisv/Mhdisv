Women's Dresses
Ready to rock a glamorous look for your next big event or bring a little beauty to your next brunch date? Shop women’s dresses at étoile’s from brands like Calvin Klein, GUESS, Alex Evenings, Lauren Ralph Lauren, and more. The styles are endless, the prints are gorgeous and the fits are dreamier than ever with a wide array of silhouettes designed for every shape. So, consider the occasion, gather your style inspo and let’s look at some dazzling dress options.
Blushing Bridesmaid
Here comes the…bridesmaid! You’re likely on the hunt for something formal and long in length. Bridesmaid dresses are meant to be dazzling, so look for flowy metallic options or dresses designed from airy chiffon. Little details like cold-shoulder cutouts, fluttery sleeves or tied straps make beautiful accents for the big day.
Cocktail Hour
For a more semi-formal occasion like cocktail hour, cocktail dresses in relaxed silhouettes and prints are perfect options. Look for pretty picks like embroidered eyelets on an A-line silhouette, peplum waistlines on fitted sheaths or faux-wrap dresses with halter necklines. A sleeveless dress with a blooming print is always a timeless choice.
Homecoming Gold
Celebrate in a homecoming dress that does all the talking. Think high-low gowns with shoulder-baring necklines, one-shoulder mini dresses or a midi dress with flouncy ruffles. With fun and fresh details like that, you’ll be the life of the party.
Maternity Looks
For a maternity dress that celebrates your beautiful bump, we have you covered. From short shift dresses to flowy maxi looks, we have a maternity dress that’s ready for your close-up.
Prom Party
Get ready to party in prom dresses ready to rock. If you want glitter and sequins, we have plenty. If you want strapless gowns with thigh-high slits, we got you. From two-piece fit & flare dresses to cut-out minis with metallic shine, your prom look will be one for the books.
Styles Galore
No matter the occasion, we have an endless array of silhouettes. With polished shirtdresses, casual midi dresses and everything in between, there is a fit and style for everyone. Shop women’s dresses, available at étoile’s .
